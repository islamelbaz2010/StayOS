# TRUST_FRAMEWORK.md

As Chief Customer Experience Officer, I am establishing the StayOS Trust Framework. Trust is not a feature; it is the currency of our ecosystem. We are moving beyond static reviews to active, human-centric security.

## 1. Identity & Verification (The "Zero-Ghost" Protocol)

* **Universal ID Verification:** Every user (Guest, Host, Manager) must complete mandatory biometric identity verification using AI-assisted national ID or passport scanning within 60 seconds.
* **Verified Profiles:** No anonymous accounts. Every profile is cross-referenced against government and social data to ensure authenticity.
* **Background Checks:** Automated, real-time background screening for all hosts and property managers before property activation.

## 2. Financial Trust (The "Vault" Escrow)

* **Full Escrow Protection:** Guest payments are held in a secure, third-party escrow account. Funds are only released to the host 24 hours after a successful, verified check-in.
* **Instant Refunds:** In cases of property misrepresentation or safety violations, refunds are processed and released back to the guest within 24 hours.
* **Fraud Zero-Tolerance:** Automated transaction monitoring blocks any suspicious payment patterns within 5 seconds of trigger.

## 3. Physical & Social Safety

* **The "Guardian" Panic Button:** A one-tap emergency feature in the app, accessible 24/7, that alerts StayOS response teams, local security, and pre-set emergency contacts simultaneously.
* **Women-Only Verified Filters:** A dedicated filter to show only properties verified as "Safe-Space," featuring secure, high-quality locks, verified female hosts, and reviewed neighborhood safety.
* **Family & Child Safety Standards:** Mandatory safety checklist for hosts (e.g., child-proofing, carbon monoxide detectors) verified via photo-upload before the property can be listed for families.
* **Foreign Tourist Assistance:** Multilingual, 24/7 support providing local navigation help, trusted transport links, and emergency assistance in the guest's native language.

## 4. Integrity & Transparency

* **Immutable Reviews:** Reviews can only be submitted by verified guests who completed a stay. The system prevents "review bombing" or fraudulent endorsements.
* **Resolution SLA:** Any dispute between host and guest regarding property quality or conduct must be resolved by a human agent within 15 minutes of initial contact.
* **Emergency Infrastructure:** Every property must provide a physical, printed "StayOS Emergency Guide" in the room, containing local police, hospital, and emergency consulate contacts.

## 5. Dispute Resolution Protocol

* **Evidence-Based Decisions:** Disputes are resolved using time-stamped, geo-tagged photo/video evidence uploaded by the actor within 10 seconds of an incident.
* **Impartial Mediation:** A dedicated "Trust & Safety" council reviews all contested claims, ensuring no actor has an unfair advantage.

---

*StayOS is built on the belief that human safety and financial integrity are the baseline requirements, not premium upgrades.*
