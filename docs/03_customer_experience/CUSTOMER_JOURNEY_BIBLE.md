## 7. Customer Journey Scorecard

*Current System Baseline Score: **62/100***

### Weak Points & Improvement Suggestions

1. **Arrival Friction (Score 40):** The transition from travel to entry is currently high-stress.
* *Improvement:* Biometric/One-tap mobile entry; 100% automated instructions via WhatsApp 24 hours prior.


2. **Support Response (Score 35):** Human agents are currently reactive, not proactive.
* *Improvement:* Real-time, multi-channel support that senses frustration (via NLP) and escalates to a human instantly.


3. **Post-Stay Engagement (Score 50):** The loop is currently a dead end.
* *Improvement:* Automate "Welcome Back" offers based on specific stay history (e.g., "Ready for another sunset view?").



---

### Emotional & Decision Logic

* **Why Continue?** Trust, perceived value, and lack of friction.
* **Why Cancel?** Lack of transparency, hidden fees, slow communication.
* **Why Return?** Emotional connection, recognition of preferences, seamless experience.
* **Why Leave?** Unresolved disappointment, better competitor UX, privacy concerns.

---

### Opportunities for WOW

* **The "Zero-Step" Arrival:** The door unlocks as they walk up. No keys, no codes, no apps to open.
* **The Proactive Fix:** Before the guest notices the AC is humming, the Property Manager has already dispatched a technician and updated the guest via WhatsApp.
* **The Personal Touch:** A gift waiting based on prior profile data (e.g., preferred coffee, local wine).
