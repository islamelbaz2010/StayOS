---

## METHODOLOGY

Every business is built on a stack of assumptions. The assumptions at the bottom of the stack are the most dangerous — if they're wrong, everything above them collapses. This document catalogs every assumption, rates its danger level, and specifies how to validate or kill it before investing resources.

**Danger Rating:**
- 🔴 **CRITICAL** — If wrong, company fails. Validate before anything else.
- 🟡 **IMPORTANT** — If wrong, major pivot required. Validate in Month 1–3.
- 🟢 **MANAGEABLE** — If wrong, product/strategy adjustment needed. Validate by Month 6.

## ASSUMPTION STACK ANALYSIS

The most dangerous assumptions form a dependency chain. If any link in this chain breaks, all assumptions above it collapse:

```
FOUNDATION: Egypt has significant unmet accommodation demand (#1)
    ↓
Egyptian supply wants to list on a platform (#26)
    ↓
Payments can be processed reliably (#4, financial section)
    ↓
Trust can be established faster than competitors (#9, competitive)
    ↓
CAC/LTV ratio becomes positive within 18 months (#76, #77)
    ↓
Series A is achievable at Month 24 (#85)
    ↓
GCC expansion is viable with existing capital (#13, market)
    ↓
StayOS becomes the regional leader (#90)
```

**If FOUNDATION is wrong — the company should not exist.**

**If any other link is wrong — a major pivot is required.**
