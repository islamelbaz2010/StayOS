## FRAMEWORK

Donald Rumsfeld's taxonomy of unknowns applies:
- **Known knowns:** Facts we know we know
- **Known unknowns:** Things we know we don't know (this document)
- **Unknown unknowns:** Things we don't know we don't know (can't be documented, but can be reduced by more customer research)

This document covers **Known Unknowns only.** The act of completing the Required Validations (Document 16) will convert most Known Unknowns into Known Knowns.

## THE MOST DANGEROUS UNKNOWN

**MU-02: What percentage of Egyptian travelers book accommodation online vs. offline/WhatsApp?**

If less than 20% of Egyptian accommodation bookings are (or can be) digital, the total addressable market for a digital platform is too small for venture scale. This single data point determines whether StayOS is a viable VC-backed company or a lifestyle business.

**Resolve this first, before any other activity.**
