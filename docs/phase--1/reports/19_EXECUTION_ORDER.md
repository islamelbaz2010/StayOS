## PRIME DIRECTIVE

> **Do the right thing in the right order. Doing the wrong thing faster does not help.**

The most common founder mistake is executing in the wrong sequence. Specifically:
- Building before validating
- Hiring before proving the model
- Scaling before achieving liquidity
- Fundraising before having evidence

This document provides the correct sequence. Follow it. Do not skip steps.

### Week 2 Actions

**Supply interviews: 10 of 30 (Egyptian property owners)**

Interview framework:
1. How many properties do you rent? Where?
2. Which platforms do you list on currently?
3. What is your monthly rental income?
4. What is your biggest frustration with existing platforms?
5. What would you pay for better? (Test specific features)
6. What commission rate is acceptable to you?
7. Would you accept instant booking?
8. How many guest incidents have you had in the last 12 months?

**Demand interviews: 10 of 50 (Egyptian domestic travelers)**

Interview framework:
1. How often do you travel within Egypt for leisure?
2. How did you book your last accommodation?
3. What went wrong? What frustrated you?
4. What would make you trust a new booking platform?
5. How much do you typically spend per night?
6. Would you pay 15% more for a verified, trusted booking? Why/why not?

**Research deadlines:**
- Market size research: North Coast and Red Sea rental market (MU-04, MU-08)
- Competitor analysis: Booking.com Egypt vs. Airbnb Egypt (CU-01, CU-05)
- Payment integration timeline research: actual Fawry integration duration

## WEEK 5–8: MANUAL PILOT (10 Real Transactions)

### Week 5 Setup

- [ ] Contact top 20 pilot host candidates from interview pool
- [ ] Offer: "I will help you get your first 3 bookings. No commission. Just let me learn."
- [ ] Verify 20 pilot hosts: video call walking through property
- [ ] Create simple listing sheet (Google Slides or basic website): 20 properties, photos, prices
- [ ] Set up pilot booking process: WhatsApp as booking intake + manual spreadsheet
- [ ] Set up pilot payment process: bank transfer + receipt confirmation
- [ ] Write dispute resolution policy (1 page): "If anything goes wrong, here's what we do"

### Week 5–8: Execute 10 Manual Transactions

**Goal: 10 completed bookings, with 5 checked out**

For each booking:
1. Guest sends WhatsApp inquiry
2. Founder reviews and confirms availability with host
3. Founder sends payment instructions
4. Guest pays
5. Founder confirms booking to both parties
6. Check-in: founder confirms successful
7. Checkout: founder requests feedback from both
8. Document: What went wrong? What was surprisingly easy?

**Track for every booking:**
- Time spent per booking (minutes)
- Issues encountered
- Guest source (how they found us)
- Host response time
- What the guest valued most
- What the guest complained about
- Would they book again?

### Week 8 Assessment

Review all 10 transactions and answer:

1. What is the actual CAC for these 10 bookings?
2. What is the actual time cost per booking?
3. What are the top 3 operational problems?
4. What is the guest NPS (simple 1–10 question)?
5. What is the host NPS?
6. Are there 3+ guests who said they would definitely book again?
7. Is there evidence of a specific, repeatable problem that we solved?

**Week 8 Gate:**
- YES (pass all 4 validation gates from Document 16 Sets A & B) → Proceed
- CONDITIONAL → Identify which gates failed; extend pilot
- NO → Major pivot or stop
