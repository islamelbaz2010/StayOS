## PHASE 0 CRITICAL CONSTRAINTS

**Constraint 1: No hiring before company formation is complete**
Egyptian labor law creates obligations from the moment of employment. Do not hire before the company is legally registered.

**Constraint 2: No product development before payment integration**
Payments are the critical path. If payments aren't live, no transaction can occur. Development that doesn't unlock the payment integration is not critical path.

**Constraint 3: No public launch before 100 verified listings**
A public launch with fewer than 100 quality listings destroys the first impression permanently. Do not launch to the public until liquidity threshold is met.

**Constraint 4: No press before trust infrastructure**
Do not seek press coverage or viral moments before: identity verification, payment escrow, dispute resolution, and 24/7 Arabic support are all live.

**Constraint 5: No GCC expansion planning before Egypt launch**
Phase 0 is Egypt only. GCC expansion is Phase 2+. Any Phase 0 energy spent on GCC is Phase 0 energy not spent on Egypt — and Egypt must be won first.
