## RISK RATING FRAMEWORK

**Probability:**
- P1 = Low (< 25%)
- P2 = Medium (25–50%)
- P3 = High (50–75%)
- P4 = Very High (> 75%)

**Impact:**
- I1 = Minor (recoverable, < $50K impact)
- I2 = Moderate ($50K–$200K, requires pivot)
- I3 = Severe ($200K–$1M, existential threat)
- I4 = Catastrophic (company-ending)

**Priority = P × I:**
- P1I1 = LOW
- P2I2 = MEDIUM
- P3I3 = HIGH
- P4I4 = CRITICAL

**Status:**
- OPEN = Unmitigated
- MONITORING = Being tracked
- MITIGATED = Action in place
- CLOSED = No longer applicable

1. Ghost listings (MKT-002) → Trust incident
2. Trust incident (MKT-005) → Viral negative press
3. Viral negative press → Supply churn (MKT-003)
4. Supply churn → Demand evaporates
5. Demand evaporates → Revenue fails → Series A fails (FIN-003)
6. Series A fails → Shutdown

**Prevention:** Trust infrastructure must be the #1 engineering and operations priority from Day 1.
